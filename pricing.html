Commission Price Guide

Thank you for showing interest in my plushie commissions!
I mainly offer My Little Pony and Pokémon plushies, but I can basically make anything you wish for as long as I feel comfortable with the design and it does not violate any copyright issues.
Prices shown below are in € (EURO). Please use a currency converter (e.g. type "euro to dollar" into Google search) if you wish to know the price in your currency.
My prices exclude shipping costs, but they do include taxes and PayPal fees! I ship internationally, but do keep in mind that I am located in Europe and shipping outside of Europe may take around 2-4 weeks due to customs/border controls. I have listed the shipping fees here.

What increases the price?

The following price examples apply to standing style ponies between 20-50cm. For mini and beanie style ponies the following additions will cost only half of the amount listed below.

  • Unicorn horn: +20
  • Special horn: +25
  • Closed wings: +40
  • Open wings: +60
  • Multiple body colors: +50 per color
  • Multicolored mane: +30 per color
  • Gradients: +50-100 (= custom-dyed fabric)
  • Fluffy hooves (e.g. G5 styled ponies): +30
  • Complex body markings: depends on complexity and amount
  • Clothing: +25-100

    • Hat (+35)
    • Shirt (+50)
    • Jacket (+70)
    • Body Armor (+100)
  • Accessories: +20-50

✦ Shipping Costs

I am located in Austria (Europe) and I ship internationally. Shipping within Europe usually takes less than a week, whereas shipping to other continents may take longer due to customs and border controls (2-4 weeks).
While I am trying my best to ship my plushies as fast as possible and always choose fast & insured shipping, the time my packages spend at the customs is sadly completely out of my control.

Shipping costs are as follows:

  • Austria - 5€
  • Europe - 10€
  • USA/Canada - 20€
  • Rest of the world - 25€

✦ FAQ

• How long does it take for you to finish my commission?

I try to finish my commissions within 2-4 weeks after accepting payment. Please keep in mind that I have a demanding little baby, so the time I have to work on orders is highly dependent on how demanding she is!

• Do you accept bulk orders? And if so, do you give discounts?

I do accept bulk orders as long as they are under 10 plushies. However, I cannot give any discounts as I work by myself and the time it takes for me to make the same plushie once, twice or even 10 times stays the same.

• Something happened and I do not have the money to commission you anymore. Do you give refunds?

As long as I have not accepted payment from you yet, you can cancel your commission any time. However, if you already paid then chances are high that I have already started working your plushie. In that case I can only refund you part of the payment. Also, I do not give refunds on finished plushies.

• I am on your waiting list. When do I need to pay?

I will message you once I am ready to work on your plushie. As soon as I have received your payment, I will start working on your order.
